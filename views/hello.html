THE ECLIPCE
"Eclipse" in Berserk is an epic saga depicting struggles, power-hungriness, and dark forces. The main protagonist, Guts, seeks revenge against his former leader Griffith, who betrayed their band. Along his journey, he encounters various dangers, demons, and his own inner demons. The richly woven plot, filled with battles, friendship, and betrayal, makes "Eclipse" a magnificent work of dark fantasy genre. In the heart of the tumultuous world of 'Eclipse', alliances shift like shadows, and every step forward brings Guts closer to the heart of darkness he seeks to confront.
